Correct!
Great job! 3.5 × 2.4 = 8.4. Remember to count the decimal places in both numbers (1 in 3.5 and 1 in 2.4) for a total of 2 decimal places in the answer.
Not Quite Right
The correct answer is 8.4. When multiplying decimals:
1) Multiply as whole numbers: 35 × 24 = 840
2) Count total decimal places (1 + 1 = 2)
3) Place the decimal: 8.40 = 8.4
